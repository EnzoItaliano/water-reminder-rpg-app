# Privacy Policy for HydroQuest 🛡️

**Last Updated:** March 27, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. **HydroQuest** is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. This policy explains in full detail how the mobile application collects, processes, and stores your data, in compliance with the Google Play Developer Policies.

---

## 1. Data Collection

HydroQuest collects only the data necessary to operate its core features. All data collection is **local-only**. The app does not transmit any of this information to external servers or cloud providers.

### 1.1 Local Data (Always Collected)

The following data is generated by your interactions and stored locally on your device storage.

| Data Type | Details |
|---|---|
| **Hydration Data** | Water intake goal (ml), number of cups logged, and timestamps of each drink within a session |
| **Session Data** | Session duration, start time, result (won/lost), difficulty level, and chosen monster ID |
| **RPG Progress** | Player level, XP, gold, unlocked monsters, and trophy history (date, monster, difficulty) |
| **User Settings** | Notification preferences and last selected monster |

We do **not** collect:
- Your name, phone number, email, or any other personal identifier
- Your device's Advertising ID or precise geolocation data
- Health or biometric data beyond what you manually enter

---

## 2. Data Processing

All core game logic runs entirely on your device. The app processes your local data to:
- Calculate your daily progress toward your water intake goal
- Trigger RPG events (battles, level-ups, gold rewards) based on your hydration habits
- Schedule internal reminder logic based on your session settings
- Display local notifications when a reminder is due or a session ends

No automated or background cloud processing occurs.

---

## 3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. This means:
- Your data **remains on your device** at all times
- It is **not synced** to any cloud account or remote server
- It is **not accessible** to any third-party service or remote tracking tool
- Uninstalling the application completely removes all associated local data

---

## 4. Data Sharing

HydroQuest does **not** sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for commercial purposes. There is no transmission of your game data or personal information. 

Specifically:
- We do **not** share data with advertisers or analytics providers
- We do **not** share data with any government or law enforcement entity
- We do **not** share data with other applications

---

## 5. Permissions

HydroQuest may request the following device permissions to function properly:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `Notifications` | Displays drink reminders and session result alerts to you |
| `Vibrate` | Provides haptic feedback during alerts or specific actions within the app |

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:
- **Local data** is managed within the app's secure storage sandbox, governed by your device's operating system (Android/iOS)
- The app does **not** execute remote code; all logic is bundled locally
- Since no data is transmitted over the internet, your game progress is completely secure from network intercepts

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

HydroQuest is safe for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data collected remains exclusively on the device.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:
- **Deletion:** You can permanently delete all local game data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing the app's data through your device settings
- **Correction:** You can modify your local game data by resetting it from within the app settings

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

As HydroQuest evolves, this policy may be updated to reflect new features (such as optional cloud integration) or legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

---

## 10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the project's GitHub repository or contact the developer directly.
